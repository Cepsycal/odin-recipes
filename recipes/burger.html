Classic Beef Burger

Nothing beats a classic beef burger, especially when made at home. This recipe offers a juicy, flavorful patty topped with melted cheddar cheese, fresh veggies, and your favorite condiments, all sandwiched between perfectly toasted buns. Whether for a backyard BBQ or a casual dinner, this burger is sure to satisfy.

Instructions

For the Patty:

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder

For the Burger:

  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices of cheddar
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • Lettuce Leaves
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise

Instructions

    Step 1: Prepare the Patties

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder. Mix until just combined; do not overwork the meat.
  2. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape each portion into a patty about 3/4 inch thick. Make a small indentation in the center of each patty with your thumb to prevent the burgers from puffing up during cooking

    3. Step 2: Cook the Patties

  3. Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the patties
  4. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side for medium doneness. Adjust the time for your preferred level of doneness
  5. During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheddar cheese on each patty to melt

    6. Step 3: Toast the Buns

  6. While the patties are cooking, lightly toast the burger buns on the grill or in a toaster until they are golden brown

    7. Step 4: Assemble the Burgers

  7. Spread a layer of mayonnaise on the bottom half of each toasted bun
  8. Place a lettuce leaf on the bottom bun, followed by a cheese-topped patty
  9. Add slices of tomato, red onion, and pickles
  10. Drizzle ketchup and mustard over the top
  11. Cover with the top half of the bun

    12. Step 5: Serving the Burger

  12. Serve the burgers hot with your favorite sides like dries, coleslaw, or a salad

Enjoy your delicious homemade classic beef burger!
