Burger

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices of cheddar
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • Lettuce Leaves
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise

In a large bowl, combine the ground beef with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder. Mix until just combined; do not overwork the meat.

Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape each portion into a patty about 3/4 inch thick. Make a small indentation in the center of each patty with your thumb to prevent the burgers from puffing up during cooking

Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the patties

Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side for medium doneness. Adjust the time for your preferred level of doneness

During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheddar cheese on each patty to melt

While the patties are cooking, lightly toast the burger buns on the grill or in a toaster until they are golden brown

Spread a layer of mayonnaise on the bottom half of each toasted bun

Place a lettuce leaf on the bottom bun, followed by a cheese-topped patty

Add slices of tomato, red onion, and pickles

Drizzle ketchup and mustard over the top

Cover with the top half of the bun

Serve the burgers hot with your favorite sides like dries, coleslaw, or a salad

Enjoy your delicious homemade classic beef burger!
