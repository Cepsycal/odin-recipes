  • 4 bone-in, skin on chicken drumsticks

In a large bowl, mix the buttermilk and hot sauce.

Add the chicken pieces, ensuring they are fully submerged in the buttermilk mixture. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, preferably overnight.

In another large bowl, combine the flour, salt, black pepper, paprika, garlic powder, and onion powder.

Remove the chicken from the buttermilk mixture, letting any excess drip off.

Dredge each piece of chicken in the seasoned flour mixture, pressing lightly to ensure a good coating. Place the coated chicken on a wire rack and let it sit for about 15 minutes to set the coating.

In a large, deep skillet or Dutch oven, heat about 1 inch of vegetable oil to 325째F (165째C).

Carefully add the chicken pieces to the hot oil, frying in batches to avoid overcrowding.

Fry the chicken for 12-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until golden brown and crispy. Ensure the internal temperature reaches 165째F (74째C).

Transfer the fried chicken to a wire rack to drain any excess oil.

In a small saucepan, heat the lard or vegetable oil over medium heat until melted and hot.

Serve the Nashville Hot Chicken on slices of white bread with dill pickle slices on top.

Enjoy your spicy and delicious Nashville Hot Chicken!
