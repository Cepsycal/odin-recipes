Nashville Fried Chicken Recipe

Experience the fiery flavors of the South with this Nashville Hot Chicken recipe. Known for its crispy exterior and spicy kick, this dish is a must-try for heat seekers. Marinated in buttermilk, perfectly fried, and coated in a signature hot sauce, this chicken is served on a bed of white bread with dill pickles for an authentic Nashville experience.

Ingredients:

    For the Chicken:

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 4 bone-in, skin on chicken drumsticks
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 1 tablespoon hot sauce
  • 2 cups all-purpouse flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • A lot of vegetable oil

    • For the Hot Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup lard or vegetable oil
  • 3 tablespoons cayenne pepper
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

    • For Serving:

  • Sliced White Bread
  • Dill Pickle Slices

Instructions:

    Step 1: Marinate the Chicken

  1. In a large bowl, mix the buttermilk and hot sauce.
  2. Add the chicken pieces, ensuring they are fully submerged in the buttermilk mixture. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, preferably overnight.

    3. Step 2: Prepare the Flour Mixture

  3. In another large bowl, combine the flour, salt, black pepper, paprika, garlic powder, and onion powder.

    4. Step 3: Dredge the Chicken

  4. Remove the chicken from the buttermilk mixture, letting any excess drip off.
  5. Dredge each piece of chicken in the seasoned flour mixture, pressing lightly to ensure a good coating. Place the coated chicken on a wire rack and let it sit for about 15 minutes to set the coating.

    6. Step 4: Fry the Chicken

  6. In a large, deep skillet or Dutch oven, heat about 1 inch of vegetable oil to 325째F (165째C).
  7. Carefully add the chicken pieces to the hot oil, frying in batches to avoid overcrowding.
  8. Fry the chicken for 12-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until golden brown and crispy. Ensure the internal temperature reaches 165째F (74째C).
  9. Transfer the fried chicken to a wire rack to drain any excess oil.

    10. Step 5: Prepare the Hot Sauce

  10. In a small saucepan, heat the lard or vegetable oil over medium heat until melted and hot.
  11. Serve the Nashville Hot Chicken on slices of white bread with dill pickle slices on top.

Enjoy your spicy and delicious Nashville Hot Chicken!
